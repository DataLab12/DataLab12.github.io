Scaling Social Balance Theory for Social and Content Network Analysis

A Data Science Perspective

Social balance theory defines balance or imbalance of sentiment relation in relations with social network theory. Sentiments can result in the emergence of two groups, where discrepancy can exist between two groups. Social and content networks model the community dynamics. How to describe and identify inequity and bias in that community? Innovative scalable graph processing algorithm that scales social balance theory to automatically quantify disbalance and to predict outcomes in social and content networks.
