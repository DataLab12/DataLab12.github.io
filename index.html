Texas Department of Transportation (TxDOT) awarded $451,875 for a 3-year project "Artificial Intelligence for Pavement Condition Assessment from 2D/3D Surface Images". Dr. Feng Wang, Civil Engineering is the PI, and Dr. Tešić is a co-PI. Within this project, the Data Lab will develip object localization from top down multi spectral imagery at scale.

Data Lab received two RTX A6000 48GB GPUs as a gift from NVIDIA. The gift will support the research in developing a high-recall low-noise deep learning system for localizing objects from 20,000 x 20,000 pixel overhead images at various scales from a variable distance.
