Object Localization and Identification in Maritime Domain

Motivation: Maritime domain imagery has different properties than consumer imagery. How applicable are state-of-art machine learning method to this domain for piracy detection. Approach: Proposed and deployed multiple solutions for high-confidence maritime object detection, small sample learning schema for maritime object identification given few samples, and maritime activity modeling.

Identifying Structures in Aerial Imagery

Motivation: How to utilize open source city data for AI systems? Approach: Transform open source City Data to machine vision training data. Adapt and train deep network to identify structures of varying size in overhead imagery. Adapt approach to work on edge, and deploy it on Nvidia Jetson TX2 mobile platform.

Sclalable Health Data Integration and Analysis

Motivation: Health data is unstructured, noisy, and incomplete. How can data science help unlock the information? Approach Propose to review the approaches fromd ata science perspective and utilize data reduction and network techniques to scale integration and analysis of biological and clinical data.

Scaling Social Balance Theory for Social and Content Netowork Analysis

Motivation: Social Networks model the community dynamics. How to describe and identify inequity and bias in that community? Innovative scalable graph processing algorithm that scales social balance theory to automatically quantify disbalance and to predict outcomes in social and content networks.
