Research opportunities
Ph.D. student should apply through the TXST Portal and specify Dr. Tešić as the potential advisor.
Undergraduate and M.Sc. students: please contact Dr. Tešić only after you have: (1) Took at least one course Dr. Tešić instructed and placed in top 10 in the class; (2) Completed CS4347/5395 under Dr. Tešić’s supervision, and demonstrated meaningful progress on the project.
